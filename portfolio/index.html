About Me

As a self-taught front-end developer, I've built my skills through hands-on experience and a passion for web development. I discovered my passion for web development whilst taking time out from my HR career to travel. From starting out developing a static blog site from scratch to share writing with friends, to building fully interactive web applications, I've been able to apply my problem-solving skills and prioritise user experience to bring my ideas to life.
